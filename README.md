Piltaland is a fictional federation of 16 autonomous states, inspired by the UAE model. The Federal Unified Government manages wide-area infrastructure (such as railway licenses), while each state maintains its own governance through dedicated repositories.

### Historical Context

Originally an English-speaking region, Piltaland was once governed by an Eastern Empire (Japan). This legacy has deeply embedded Japanese language into our railway terminology and administrative systems, making us a bilingual nation where English and Japanese coexist in official documentation.

### Governance Philosophy

**Law as Code** - All national laws, regulations, and infrastructure specifications are version-controlled in GitHub. Every change goes through:
1. Pull Request submission
2. Community review
3. Federal approval
4. Merge and deployment

### For Railway Operators

To apply for a railway license:

1. Fork `/railway-standards`
2. Create your application in `/applications/[your-company-name].md`
3. Include:
   - Proposed route and gauge
   - Technical specifications
   - Safety plan
   - Environmental impact assessment
4. Submit Pull Request with tag `railway-license-application`
5. Engage with Federal Railway Board during review
